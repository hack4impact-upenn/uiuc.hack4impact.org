Software for Nonprofits

We are a team of UIUC students who create robust software for social good. We believe that our engineering skill sets do not render us useless in addressing social injustices. Instead, they empower us to build a better society together.

Our Work

Each semester, we select 3-5 nonprofits and provide student teams of 4-7 with the opportunity to build a great product that solves a core need. In just 1 year, we've created a management system for a tutoring program between Cambodian students and English tutors, an interactive African American history educational tool for Florida students, and much more!
